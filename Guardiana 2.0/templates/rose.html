Rose



          


SCIENTIFIC NAME : Rosa

COMMON NAME : Rose

ORIGIN : Asia

SCIENTIFIC CLASSIFICATION:
  • Kingdom: Plantae
  • Division: Angiosperms
  • Order: Rosales
  • Family: Rosaceae
  • Genus: Rosa
CULTIVATION TIPS:
Sunlight :
1. Roses thrive in full sunlight.
2. They should receive at least 6 hours of direct sunlight each day.
3. In very hot climates, some afternoon shade can help prevent heat stress.
Watering :
1. Roses prefer well-drained soil and regular watering.
2. Water deeply, ensuring the soil is moist but not waterlogged.
3. Avoid overhead watering to prevent fungal diseases; water at the base of the plant.
Temperature :
1. Roses grow best in temperatures between 60째F (15째C) and 75째F (24째C).
2. They can tolerate higher temperatures if watered adequately but struggle in extreme cold.
3. In colder climates, provide winter protection or bring potted roses indoors.
Soil Type:
1. Roses prefer rich, well-draining soil.
2. A slightly acidic to neutral pH range of 6.0 to 7.0 is ideal.
3. Soil should be enriched with organic matter, such as compost or well-rotted manure.
4. Ensure good drainage to prevent root rot.

USES:
  • Ornamental: Popular in gardens and landscaping for their beautiful and fragrant flowers.
  • Perfume: Used in the production of rose oil and rose water.
  • Culinary: Rose petals and hips are used in cooking, teas, and preserves.
  • Medicinal: Rose hips are rich in vitamin C and used in herbal remedies.
  • Skincare: Roses are used in products for their moisturizing and anti-inflammatory properties.
